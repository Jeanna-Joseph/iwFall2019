About postento

As a platform, we strive to connect student influencers on Instagram with local restaurants.

Restaurants can gain visibility in campus communities and students can enjoy free meals in exchange for their content.

Sign up if you are a restaurant near a university, or a student with an avid Instagram following!

Students use our service free of charge.

Restaurants pay an annual subscription fee of $50.00 to use the service. For 12 months of incredible content and building relationships within the campus community, we at postento think it's worth it.
